We also have one of the coolest offices! Wait till you see our huge salt water aquarium!

      History

      Established in 2007.

      When your injury or illness is urgent, but not life threatening, Urgent Care at Newport Center is the place you need. This includes infections, injuries, cold, flu, fever, cuts, minor burns, bites, broken bones, sore throat, vomiting, sprains, etc. Any injury or illnesses which is not life threatening and would require a trip to the emergency room can be done at our facility, faster and cheaper.

      Our Medical Director is Dr. Bryan Doonan. He is also a Family Practitioner and can be seen as your Primary Care Physician.  Dr. Doonan was formerly a professional surfer and is currently the traveling physician for the ASP  World Surfing Tour.  

      Appointments are not needed but encouraged for shorter wait times. We are dedicated to our patients health and are innovative in growing and improving our facility as well. We give you access to all of your medical history with us, such as Rx's, Vaccines, and test results in real time. All in a safe electronic portal that only you have access to.
